Your pet's digital identity.

Built on the blockchain, Petfolio is a universal ID that holds your pet's entire life story.

Health Records

We store your pet’s identity, health info and life history securely on the blockchain, so it's yours to take everywhere: check in quickly to boarding houses, pet classes, and onto the plane.

Adoption

With full access to a pet's history, animal shelters can better care for rescued animals, recommend adoptions, and transfer records between vets and service providers.

Care Network

Connect with your pet's previous owners. Find out about its family history, breed characteristics, and preventative health recommendations. Create a network of care for your pet.

For Vets

Quick and secure transfer of medical files between vet offices and pet services.

  • Eric
    Greenwich Village Animal Hospital, NYC

    "This is a really cool idea - information exchange is a huge part of our business, it happens multiple times a day, and if there was a way we could enter information securely so that labs, specialists, hospitals, other vets, and pet parents could access the information, it would really improve our current system of manually responding to individual requests through email, fax, and outdated software that doesn’t allow communication among all of those entities.”

  • Dr. Estie Arkin
    Last Chance Animal Rescue, Long Island

    "I have fostered hundreds of dogs that come from all over the United States. When I take home a rescued dog, I wonder: how old is this dog? What is the breed? Where is it from? ...Access to these medical records would be so helpful. Vets and rescue organizations would love it...It would help us take better care of a lot of animals. "

  • Earl
    Veterinary Care Center, Los Angeles

    "We’re currently using software that hasn’t been updated since 2007 and it doesn’t allow us to fulfill all the info requests we get, we’re still faxing documents on a daily basis. And follow up, and resending… it’s just totally disorganized, and I wish there was a one stop shop because it takes up so much time."
